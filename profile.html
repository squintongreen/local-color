in color

Local Color Creations is home to unique, vibrant, and sophisticated hand-made jewelry. It’s made for those who find comfort and confidence in color, and perfect for anyone who views their personal style as a way to express their originality and authentic beauty.

Local Color specializes in balancing bold hues, rhythmic patterns, and rich texture - allowing for endless inspiration to incorporate these one-of-a-kind pieces into an ever-evolving wardrobe. Combining simple materials with age-old techniques, jewelry from each collection rings both timeless and trendy.

Jacqueline Crowther has her Bachelor’s of Science in urban policy studies, planning & economic development from Georgia State University.

Her passion for big cities, distant places and the colorful people that make them great inspires and informs everything she does, including making jewelry that exudes the vibrance, versatility and vivacity of a modern global citizen.
