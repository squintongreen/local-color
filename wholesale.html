Interested in carrying Local Color's original, hand-made accessories in your online or brick-and-mortar store? I'd be thrilled to discuss my terms and figure out what Local Color can offer your customers.

While some pieces are one-of-a-kind or require use of materials available only in limited quantities, Local Color has plenty of bold, vibrant wholesale offerings. From various styles of the African block print fabric wrap bangles to the modern, pastel resin rings, and much more. For the current line and price sheets, as well as full terms, please fill out the inquiry form or send e-mails directly to info@localcolorcreations.com.

  • Domestic orders are shipped using USPS Priority Mail Flat Rate (costs between $20 - $30)
  • Purchases greater than $350 get free shipping!
  • Orders must be cancelled within 7 days of placement to receive a full refund.
  • Qualifying damaged products may be returned for full refund (less the cost of shipping) within 5 days of receipt.
  • All first orders must be paid through PayPal or Etsy Direct Checkout. All major credit cards accepted.
  • Order shipped after payment received.
  • Net terms negotiable for qualified, reliable buyers.
