about local color

Local Color was born out of a need to share my passions and rediscover my own creativity. This shop is my vehicle for combining my love of color with my endless fascination with cultures and places near and far.

We all have a unique perspective to share. By combining techniques and materials used for generations with my own modern and playful style, I hope to create accessories that transport you to a particular place or feeling.

I want anyone wearing jewelry I made to feel not just pretty, but powerful. Wearing it, I hope you can confront the challenges and responsibilities life throws at you daily with confidence.
